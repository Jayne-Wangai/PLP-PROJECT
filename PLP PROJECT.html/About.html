History

Golden Bliss Spa is a high end Spa founded in 2021 in Kileleshwa
It later expanded by opening its branches to other towns
like:Nairobi Cbd, Lavington and Westlands
The Spa offers a range of services from massage, hairdos, haircuts, waxing etc.
Its main goal is to offer quality services at an affordable price

Online Booking System

Due to the relentless support from our customers we have come up with an online booking system
The main aim of this system is to reduce the wait time for our customers when they visit our spa
This system will enable them to book the exact service they want and also state the time that they
will be arriving. This will give the spa ample time to prepare for the service so that when the
customer arrives he/she wont have to wait for long
This system will also enhance their experience
